SPINNAKER

The Savings for the Poor Innovation and Knowledge Network

The Savings for the Poor Innovation and Knowledge Network (SPINNAKER) aggregates, standardizes and highlights the landscape of information and data on savings products for the poor by coordinating with thinkers and doers in the field. Our database, resource library and community space are the product of the innovative work of leaders in the savings for the poor field.
